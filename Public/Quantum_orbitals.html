📘 QUANTUM ORBITALS - User Guide

Welcome to the 3D Quantum Orbitals Laboratory! Explore the mathematical probability clouds and nodes of Hydrogen and Hydrogen-like atoms in full interactive 3D.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Principal ($n$): Defines the energy shell (Size/Bound).
  • Azimuthal ($l$): Defines the subshell shape (s, p, d, f). Note that $l$ is strictly capped at $n-1$.
  • Magnetic ($m_l$): Defines orbital orientation in space. Restricted from $-l$ to $+l$.

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Probability Cloud Shapes: Learn why quantum orbitals take on spherical, dumbbell, cloverleaf, and torus shapes in 3D.
  • Quantum Boundary States: Understand the significance of principal, azimuthal, and magnetic quantum numbers.
  • Radial & Angular Nodes: Observe the visual boundaries where the probability density $|\psi|^2$ becomes exactly zero.
