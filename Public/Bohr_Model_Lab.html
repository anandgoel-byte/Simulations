📘 BOHR MODEL - User Guide

Welcome to the Bohr Model Virtual Laboratory! Explore the quantized mechanics of Hydrogen and Hydrogen-like atoms.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Atomic Number (Z): Change the central nucleus. Notice how a stronger positive charge pulls the orbits closer (Radius $\propto 1/Z$) and forces the electron to move faster (Velocity $\propto Z$).
  • Energy Level (n): Move the electron to higher orbits. Observe the massive increase in physical distance (Radius $\propto n^2$) and the drastic drop in orbital frequency ($\propto 1/n^3$).
  • Live Telemetry: The dashboard calculates real-world values in Ångströms, eV, and Hz based on the established Bohr equations.

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Quantization of Angular Momentum: Understand why electrons can only reside in discrete, non-radiating Bohr orbits.
  • Atomic Spacings & Energy: Learn how varying $Z$ and $n$ alters the radius, velocity, frequency, and total energy of the atom.
  • Quantum Transitions: Visualize how absorption/emission of color-specific photons corresponds to transitions between energy levels.
