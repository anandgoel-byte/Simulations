📘 3D GALVANIC CELL PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the 3D Galvanic Cell Virtual Laboratory! Explore electrochemistry by constructing different batteries and watching them run in real-time 3D.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Electrode Selection: Mix and match Anodes (Zinc, Iron) and Cathodes (Copper, Silver) to see how Standard Cell Potential ($E^\circ_{cell}$) changes.
  • Concentration Sliders: Alter the molarity of the aqueous solutions. Notice how non-standard conditions shift the voltage via the Nernst Equation!
  • Run Cell: Click ▶ to close the circuit. Watch the Anode geometrically dissolve, the Cathode grow, and concentrations shift dynamically until the battery dies ($E_{cell} = 0$).
  • Camera: Click and drag on the 3D viewport to orbit the apparatus. Scroll to zoom in and out.
