📘 GEOMETRY PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Isolated Trigonometry Simulator. By isolating each function, you can clearly see how the angle dictates its exact physical length on the unit circle.

Reading the Map

  • Sine & Cosine: The basic $x$ and $y$ distances inside the circle. Bounded between -1 and 1.
  • Tangent: Imagine a solid wall at the right edge ($x=1$). The tangent is the height where the extended radius hits this wall.
  • Secant: The total length of the "laser beam" from the origin to that same wall ($x=1$).
  • Cotangent: Put a ceiling at the top edge ($y=1$). The horizontal distance along the ceiling to where the radius hits is the Cotangent.
  • Cosecant: The total length of the beam from the origin hitting the ceiling ($y=1$).
