📘 ELECTROPHYSIOLOGY PROTOCOLS - User Guide

Welcome to the Neuron Action Potential & Electrophysiology Virtual Laboratory! Explore cellular ion flows, channel gates, and refractory balance.

🎯 Core Operations

  • Inject Stimulus Current: Fires a depolarizing current pulse based on selected intensity ($I_{stim}$).
  • Parameter Syncing: Adjust Stimulus Intensity synchronously using either the slider or manual input box.
  • Myelin Sheath Configuration: Toggle between Unmyelinated and Myelinated axons. Myelin isolates the internode, decreasing capacitance and forcing high-speed saltatory jumps.
  • Tabbed Viewports: Toggle between **Axon Chamber** (shows phospholipid bilayer gates, and ionic particles flows) and **Oscilloscope Plot** (live action potential trace curve).
