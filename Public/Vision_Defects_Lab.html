📘 VISION OPTICS PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Optics & Human Anatomy Laboratory! Treat and correct structural eye defects using physical lenses.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Condition Selector: Choose between a Normal Eye, a Myopic Eye (too long, focuses early), or a Hypermetropic Eye (too short, focuses late).
  • Object Distance: Slide the tree closer to or further from the eye. Notice that a Myopic eye can actually see near objects perfectly well, but completely blurs distant objects!
  • Lens Power: Act as the optometrist. Dial in a negative (Concave) or positive (Convex) diopter value to physically bend the incoming light rays. Your goal is to get the red rays to intersect exactly on the back wall of the eye (the Retina).
  • Simulated Retina View: Watch the live HUD in the bottom right. The blurriness physically corresponds to the mathematical distance between the focal point and the retina!
