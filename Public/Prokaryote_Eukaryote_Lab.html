📘 CELL COMPARATOR PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Cellular Biology Laboratory! Compare the fundamental structures of life.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Cell Type Dropdown: Instantly morph the canvas between a simple Bacterium, a complex Animal Cell, and a rigid Plant cell.
  • Component Isolator: Use the checkboxes to strip away specific parts of the cell. Notice how toggling "Membrane Organelles" removes mitochondria and chloroplasts from Eukaryotes, but doesn't affect Prokaryotes because they never had them!
  • Telemetry Check: Observe the sidebar panel. See how the ribosome type shifts from 70S to 80S, and the DNA structure changes from Circular to Linear.

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Cell Classification: Distinguish prokaryotes from eukaryotes based on subcellular components and size telemetry.
  • Organelle Mapping: Identify which organelles (nucleus, cell wall, flagella, mitochondria) are present in plant, animal, and bacterial models.
  • Ribosomal and Genetic Divergence: Understand the evolutionary differences in ribosome sedimentation (70S vs 80S) and DNA topology.
