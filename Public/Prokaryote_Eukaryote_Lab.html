📘 CELL COMPARATOR PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Cellular Biology Laboratory! Compare the fundamental structures of life.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Cell Type Dropdown: Instantly morph the canvas between a simple Bacterium, a complex Animal Cell, and a rigid Plant cell.
  • Component Isolator: Use the checkboxes to strip away specific parts of the cell. Notice how toggling "Membrane Organelles" removes mitochondria and chloroplasts from Eukaryotes, but doesn't affect Prokaryotes because they never had them!
  • Telemetry Check: Observe the right-hand panel. See how the ribosome type shifts from 70S to 80S, and the DNA structure changes from Circular to Linear.
