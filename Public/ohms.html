📘 OHM'S LAW PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Electromagnetism Laboratory! Explore the fundamental relationship between Voltage, Current, and Resistance.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Voltage Slider: Increase the battery voltage to push more electrons through the circuit. Watch the physical speed of the electrons increase!
  • Resistance Slider: Increase the resistance to constrict the flow. You will see the current drop and the electrons slow down.
  • Test Component Toggle: Switch from an ideal Resistor to a Filament Bulb. Notice how the bulb gets hotter as voltage increases, which physically increases its resistance, causing the V-I graph to curve!
  • V-I Graph: Watch the live plotter in the left sidebar. The slope of this line is directly tied to the resistance.

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Ohm's Law Proportionality: Observe the direct linear relation between voltage ($V$) and current ($I$) in Ohmic materials.
  • Non-Ohmic Behavior: Understand how temperature increments in filaments yield dynamic resistance variations, curving the V-I slope.
  • Circuit Telemetry: Gain hands-on competence reading Ammeters, Voltmeters, and calculating electrical power dissipation ($P = VI$).
