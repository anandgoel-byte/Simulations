Welcome to the Method of Intervals Visualizer! Learn how to instantly solve complex inequalities.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Step 1: Plot all critical points (roots of numerator and denominator) on the number line.
  • Step 2: Check the leading coefficient. If positive, start the curve from the Top-Right (+). If negative, start from the Bottom-Right (-).
  • Step 3 (The Wavy Curve): Move left across the roots. If the root's power is ODD, the curve CROSSES the axis (changes sign). If the power is EVEN, it BOUNCES off the axis (keeps sign).
  • Step 4: Select the intervals! Pink shaded regions are negative ($< 0$), Emerald regions are positive ($> 0$).
