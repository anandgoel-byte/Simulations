📘 STRUCTURE COMPARATOR PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Nucleic Acid Virtual Laboratory! Explore the critical differences between the molecules of life.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Molecule Toggle: Switch the primary view between DNA and RNA using the top buttons. Watch the macrostructure completely rebuild itself!
  • Focus Level: Use the dropdown to activate the microscope.
    • Macro: View the entire helical polymer.
    • Sugar Backbone: Zooms into the pentose sugar. Pay attention to the 2' Carbon!
    • Unique Base: Zooms into the pyrimidine difference (Thymine vs. Uracil). Notice the Methyl group flag.
  • Camera Controls: Click and drag on the 3D viewport to orbit around the molecules manually.
