📘 DERIVATIVE PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Calculus Laboratory! Visualize the mechanics of finding peaks and valleys on a curve.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Coefficient Sliders ($A, B, C, D$): Alter the shape of the polynomial. Notice how changing the $A$ term radically alters the "hills" of the function.
  • Analysis Point ($x$): Slide this point along the curve. The yellow tangent line represents the first derivative (slope). Watch the telemetry update live as you move it!
  • Critical Points: Colored dots will appear automatically where the tangent is perfectly flat (slope = 0), identifying local maxima and minima.
