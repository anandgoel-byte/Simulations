🌸 Angiosperm Protocols

Welcome to the Flower Anatomy Dissection Lab. Use the tools to explore the reproductive architecture of angiosperms.

Laboratory Controls

  • Bloom Stage: Slide from 0 (Bud) to 100 (Fully Open). Notice how the green sepals form a protective shell when closed.
  • Vertical Dissection (Explode): Drag this slider to lift the four whorls apart physically. This lets you inspect the inner pistil and stamens without the petals blocking your view!
  • Visibility Toggles: Click the checkboxes at the top to completely hide or show specific organ systems (Calyx, Corolla, Androecium, Gynoecium).
