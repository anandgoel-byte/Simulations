📘 FLOWER ANATOMY PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Botanical 3D Laboratory! Explore the intricate reproductive architecture of angiosperms.

🎯 Core Controls

  • 3D Interaction: Click and drag anywhere on the dark canvas to orbit the flower. Scroll your mouse wheel to zoom in and out.
  • Biological Scanner: Hover your mouse cursor over any 3D part (Petal, Anther, Ovary, etc.). The part will highlight, and the Scanner Dashboard on the right will reveal its scientific name, whorl, and function.
  • Whorl Isolators: Use the checkboxes on the left to surgically remove layers to see the structures hidden underneath.
  • Cross-Section Slicer: Drag the red slider to dynamically slice the entire 3D flower in half! Look closely inside the swollen green Ovary to spot the tiny unfertilized ovules (seeds).
