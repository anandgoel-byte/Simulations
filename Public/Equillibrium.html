📘 CHEMICAL EQUILIBRIUM PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Le Chatelier's Principle Virtual Laboratory! Perturb a system at equilibrium and watch it chemically respond to restore balance.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Temperature ($T$): Heating the system forces it to shift backward to absorb the heat (since it is an exothermic reaction). Watch the $K_c$ drop!
  • Volume ($V$): Compressing the chamber (decreasing Volume) increases pressure. The system shifts toward $\text{N}_2\text{O}_4$ to reduce the total number of gas molecules.
  • Perturb Buttons: Inject concentrated spikes of reactants or products to disrupt the balance. The real-time chart will track the kinetic recovery path.
  • Visual Chamber: The beaker on the left visually represents the mixture. $\text{NO}_2$ is heavily colored brown, while $\text{N}_2\text{O}_4$ is colorless.
