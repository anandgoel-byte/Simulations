📘 FLEMING'S RULE PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Motor Effect Laboratory! Explore how perpendicular magnetic fields and electrical currents create physical motion.

🎯 Core Controls

  • 3D Interaction: Click and drag the dark canvas to orbit the scene. You can view the vectors from any angle to verify they are perfectly $90^\circ$ apart.
  • Vector Reversers: Click "Reverse Current" to flip the polarity of the wire. Watch the Middle Finger flip, and observe how the Red Force Thumb flips in response!
  • The Mathematical Rule: Because of the Cross Product ($\vec{F} = I \times \vec{B}$), if you reverse *both* the magnets and the current at the same time, the Force will remain exactly the same! Try it!
