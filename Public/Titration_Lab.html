📘 TITRATION PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Analytical Chemistry Laboratory! Master the delicate art of volumetric analysis.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Analyte Selection: Choose between a Strong Acid (HCl) or a Weak Acid (Acetic Acid). Notice how the starting pH and the shape of the titration curve fundamentally change!
  • Burette Stopcock Valve: Drag the red slider to open the valve and drop Base (NaOH) into the acid. Use a slow drop rate when you get close to 25 mL, because the pH will spike wildly!
  • Visual Endpoint: The liquid in the flask will change color depending on your chosen Indicator. You must choose an indicator whose color-change range perfectly catches the vertical jump of the Equivalence Point.
  • Live Graph: The Telemetry panel constantly plots your progress. Watch the classic "S" curve of the titration form in real-time.
