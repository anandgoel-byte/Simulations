📘 THE PENDULUM PROTOCOLS - User Guide

Welcome to the Simple Harmonic & Mechanical Energy Virtual Laboratory! Explore swing dynamics, conservation waves, and multi-component vector actions.

🎯 Core Functions

  • Drag and Release Bob: Click or touch the **Red Bob** directly inside the Physics Arena, drag it to any displacement angle, and let go to swing!
  • Parameter Syncing: Adjust Length ($L$), Gravity ($g$), Damping ($b$), and Mass ($m$) synchronously using sliders or manual numeric inputs.
  • Gravity Fields Presets: Instantly switch between Earth, Moon, or Jupiter environments to observe period changes.
  • Dual Viewport Visualizer: Toggle between **Physics Arena** (pendulum motion with active vector paths) and **Energy Waves** (real-time scrolling KE, PE, and Total mechanical energy trends).

📊 Vector Displays

  • Forces acting: Displays Gravity ($mg$), Tension ($T$), and net restoring force ($F_{net}$) with textbook variable labels.
  • Acceleration Components: Overlays centripetal ($a_c$) and tangential ($a_t$) acceleration vector arrows.
  • Velocity Vector: Traces the instantaneous tangential velocity arrow ($v$).
