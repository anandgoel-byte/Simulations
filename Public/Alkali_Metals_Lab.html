📘 ALKALI METALS PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Alkali Metal Reactivity Laboratory! Safely explore the violent chemistry of Group 1 elements reacting with water.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Select Metal: Choose an element from Group 1. Notice how the atomic radius increases and the ionization energy decreases as you move down the periodic table.
  • Drop Metal: Click the button to drop a chunk of the selected metal into the beaker.
  • Observe Kinetics: Watch the severity of the reaction scale perfectly with the metal's position on the periodic table. Lithium fizzes; Cesium detonates!
  • Indicator Toggle: Leave Phenolphthalein on to watch the formation of hydroxide ions physically turn the water basic (bright pink).
