📘 REACTIVITY SERIES PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Inorganic Chemistry Laboratory! Observe electron transfer through single displacement reactions.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Setup the Beaker: Select a solid metal strip and an aqueous salt solution from the left panels. Notice their initial colors.
  • Initiate Reaction: Click "Drop Metal". If the solid metal is higher on the reactivity series than the metal in the solution, a reaction will occur!
  • Visual Evidence: Watch the canvas! If a reaction occurs, the solid metal will dissolve (oxidize), and the solution metal will precipitate out as a colored coating on the strip. Simultaneously, the solution color will change as the new ions dissolve!
  • Chemical Telemetry: The right panel will break down the exact balanced chemical equation and split it into its Oxidation and Reduction half-reactions.
