📘 CELL DIVISION PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Cellular Biology Laboratory! Observe the precise dance of chromosomes during cellular reproduction.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Process Dropdown: Select between Mitosis, Meiosis I, or Meiosis II. Notice how the initial arrangement of chromosomes completely changes between processes!
  • Timeline Slider: Drag the slider to manually control time. Watch the nuclear envelope dissolve, chromosomes align at the equator, and eventually split towards the poles.
  • Quick-Jump Buttons: Instantly snap the simulation to the defining hallmarks of Prophase, Metaphase, Anaphase, or Telophase.
  • Telemetry Panel: Keep an eye on the right panel to understand exactly what is happening to the Ploidy Level ($2n \to n$) and DNA Content ($4C \to 2C \to 1C$).
