📘 RIVER CROSSING PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Relative Velocity Virtual Laboratory! Explore how independent velocity vectors combine to produce actual motion paths.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Parameter Syncing: Synced sliders or manual inputs control River Velocity ($v_r$), Boat Velocity ($v_b$), and Aim Direction ($\theta$).
  • Aim Direction: 0° points straight across (North). Negative angles point upstream (West), and positive angles point downstream (East).
  • Modern Boat Zoom: Use the new Zoom controls in the top-right of the canvas to scale the simulation up or down.
  • Live Telemetry: Track exact drift distance and resultant ground speed in the dark neumorphic dashboard as the boat navigates the current.
