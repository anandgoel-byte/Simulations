📘 BIO-SYSTEM PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Endocrine System Laboratory! Explore the chemical control and coordination of the human body.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Select Gland: Use the dropdown or click directly on any gland on the holographic silhouette to isolate it.
  • Live Telemetry: The dark dashboard reveals the specific hormones secreted by the active gland and their anatomical targets.
  • Stimulate Gland: Click this to simulate a hormone release. Watch the glowing particles travel through the bloodstream to trigger systemic or targeted effects!

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Chemical Control Mechanism: Comprehend how the endocrine system uses chemical hormones to coordinate biological activities.
  • Feedback Systems: Explain homeostatic loops and feedforward/feedback loops that control hormone release.
  • Target Organ Specificity: Visualize how hormones diffuse through the bloodstream to act on specific target receptors.
