📘 STOICHIOMETRY PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Chemistry & Conservation of Mass Lab! Practice adjusting coefficients to satisfy the universe's fundamental laws.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Coefficient Adjusters: Use the up and down arrows hovering over the chemical equation to change the number of molecules.
  • Visual Scales: Watch the animated balances at the bottom of the canvas. Your goal is to make the Left side (Reactants) exactly level with the Right side (Products) for *every single element*.
  • Atom Inventory: The left-hand panel provides a precise numerical count of the atoms. Red numbers indicate an imbalance. Green numbers indicate perfect balance.
  • Success State: When all atoms are balanced, the entire UI will light up emerald green, indicating the equation is valid!
