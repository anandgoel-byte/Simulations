Welcome to the Organic Synthesis 3D Simulator. Explore how different reagents attack carbon skeletons to form alcohols.

Reaction Mechanisms

  • Hydration vs Hydroboration: Watch how $H_2O/H^+$ attacks the double bond to place the -OH on the most substituted carbon (Markovnikov). Conversely, Boron attacks the least hindered carbon, eventually swapping to form a primary alcohol (Anti-Markovnikov).
  • Reduction: A simple hydride ($H^-$) attack on the carbonyl carbon of a ketone yields a secondary alcohol.
  • Grignard Synthesis: Watch the carbon chain from the Grignard reagent physically attach to the carbonyl carbon, transforming the ketone into a bulky tertiary alcohol.
  • Interaction: Use the "Reaction Progress" slider to physically drive the atoms through their transition states. Notice how double bonds break and reform as single bonds when new atoms attach.
