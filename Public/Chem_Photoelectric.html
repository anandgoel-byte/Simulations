📘 USER OPERATIONS GUIDE

Follow these instructions to run the Photoelectric apparatus:

  • Metal Selector: Sodium, Zinc, or Copper.
  • Frequency Slider: Set the frequency of incident photons in THz.
  • Intensity Slider: Controls photon count rate.
  • Bias Voltage: Apply positive (accelerating) or negative (decelerating) bias voltage to see its effect on photocurrent.
