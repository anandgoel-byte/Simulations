Evolutionary Shift
In primitive plants like mosses, the haploid Gametophyte is the main plant. As plants evolved towards Angiosperms, the diploid Sporophyte became completely dominant, reducing the Gametophyte to microscopic pollen and embryo sacs.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Plant Division Selector: Switch between Mosses, Ferns, and Angiosperms. Notice how the dominant (largest) stage shifts from the top half of the circle (Haploid) to the bottom half (Diploid) as plants evolve!
  • Timeline Slider: Drag the slider to move the tracker particle around the life cycle. Watch the telemetry panel to see exactly what biological process is occurring at any given moment.
  • Auto-Play: Click Auto-Play to watch the cycle progress naturally. Pay close attention to when Meiosis and Fertilization occur—they are the strict boundaries between $n$ and $2n$!
