📘 PASCAL'S TRIANGLE PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Pascal's Triangle Laboratory! Explore the deepest connections in combinatorics and algebra.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Row Slider ($n$): Adjust the active row. The active row is highlighted in blue, while the rows below it are dimmed out.
  • Combinatorics Toggle: Switch the nodes from displaying integers to displaying their formal $\binom{n}{k}$ mathematical notation.
  • Addition Tracing: Hover your mouse over any active node! If "Highlight Parent Additions" is checked, it will glow yellow and draw lines to its two parent nodes in the row above, proving the addition rule!
  • Binomial Expansion: Watch the mathematical equation at the bottom of the canvas automatically rewrite itself to perfectly match the coefficients of your selected row!
