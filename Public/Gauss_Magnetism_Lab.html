📘 MAXWELL'S PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Gauss's Law for Magnetism Simulator! Explore why magnetic field lines behave differently than electric field lines.

🎯 Core Controls

  • 3D Navigation: Click and drag on the central canvas to rotate the camera.
  • The Gaussian Surface: The glowing sphere represents an imaginary 3D boundary. Use the sliders to move it across the magnetic field or change its size.
  • Dipole vs Monopole: Switch to the Hypothetical Monopole. Notice how field lines radiate straight out, causing a Net Flux if the sphere encloses the source.
  • The Law: When the Real Dipole is active, notice that the Net Flux ALWAYS evaluates to 0.00. Every magnetic field line that enters the sphere must eventually exit it, because magnetic lines form continuous, unbroken loops!

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Gauss's Law for Magnetism: Verify visually that $\oint \vec{B} \cdot d\vec{A} = 0$ for closed dipole field loops.
  • Monopole Physics: Contrast paired dipole configurations against a hypothetical single monopole's radial divergence.
  • Flux Conservation: Understand why flux lines entering a surface must exit it to conserve magnetic charge.
