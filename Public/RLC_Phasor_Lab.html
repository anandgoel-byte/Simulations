📘 RLC PHASOR PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the AC Circuit Phasor Laboratory! Visualize the phase differences caused by reactive components.

🎯 Core Controls

  • The Sliders: Adjust Resistance ($R$), Inductance ($L$), Capacitance ($C$), and AC Frequency ($f$).
  • Resonance: Try to match the AC frequency ($f$) to the natural Resonance Frequency ($f_0$) displayed on the dashboard. Watch the phase angle drop to zero and the current spike!
  • Phasor Diagram (Top): Visualizes the signals as rotating vectors. Use the "Freeze Rotation" toggle to pause them and examine the 90° relationships between $V_R$, $V_L$, and $V_C$.
  • Waveform Graph (Bottom): Traces the actual sinusoidal time-domain signals. Notice how the peaks of the Inductor and Capacitor voltages are out of sync with the Current.
