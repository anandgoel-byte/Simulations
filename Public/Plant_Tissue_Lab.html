📘 TISSUE VIEWER PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Botanical Anatomy Laboratory! Explore the internal cross-sections of plant organs.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Specimen Selector: Instantly swap between the four major vascular cross-sections. Watch the central structural geometry change entirely between roots and stems.
  • Tissue Isolators: Check and uncheck specific tissues to isolate their locations. Non-applicable tissues (like Pith in a Dicot Root) will be automatically disabled.
  • Visual Features: Notice the "monkey face" vascular bundles in Monocot stems, the solid star-shaped Xylem core in Dicot roots, and the distinct waterproof Endodermis ring only present in roots!
