📘 PATHOGEN COMPARATOR PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Microbiology Laboratory! Explore the stark structural differences between living bacteria and viral agents.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Scale Zoom Lens: This is the most important tool. At "Normalized" (100%), both pathogens are drawn side-by-side so you can see their internal parts. Slide it to 0% to see their True Relative Size. Notice how the virus becomes a tiny, barely-visible speck!
  • Anatomy Isolator: Toggle specific biological structures on and off. Notice how unchecking "Ribosomes" removes them from the bacteria, but nothing changes on the virus (because viruses don't have ribosomes!).
  • Medical Treatment: Apply an Antibiotic. The simulator will show the bacterial cell wall rupturing while the virus remains completely untouched.
