📘 CIRCUIT TOPOLOGY PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Electrodynamics Laboratory! Explore the fundamental laws governing simple DC circuits.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Circuit Mode Toggle: Instantly switch between Series and Parallel. Watch the canvas re-wire itself! The MathJax equations on the left will automatically update to reflect the new topology.
  • Series Dynamics: When in Series, increase $R_1$. Notice how the total Resistance increases, the total Current drops, and $R_1$ "hogs" more of the voltage drop compared to $R_2$.
  • Parallel Dynamics: Switch to Parallel. Notice that the total equivalent resistance is actually *lower* than both individual resistors! Watch the electrons split at the junction: the path with the lower resistance will physically show faster electron flow.
