🌿 Evolutionary Timeline

  • Stage 1 (Gametophyte): This is the green plant you see. It is Haploid (n).
  • Stage 2 (Fertilization): Watch the rain! Bryophytes completely depend on external water for the sperm to swim to the egg.
  • Stage 3 (Sporophyte Growth): A parasitic, brownish stalk (seta) and capsule grows out of the top of the leafy plant. This is Diploid (2n).
  • Stage 4 (Meiosis): The capsule opens, releasing thousands of haploid spores.
  • Stage 5 (Protonema): Spores land and grow into green creeping filaments (protonema), restarting the cycle.
