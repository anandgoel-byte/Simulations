📘 DIHYBRID CROSS PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Mendelian Genetics Laboratory! Construct and analyze genetic probabilities through 4x4 matrices.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Cross Presets: Instantly load classic genetic scenarios like the Heterozygous Cross (9:3:3:1 ratio) or the Test Cross.
  • Parent Controls: Manually alter the alleles (Y/y and R/r) for Parent 1 and Parent 2. The system automatically calculates their possible gametes using the FOIL method.
  • The Punnett Square: Watch as the canvas automatically fills the 16 grid squares with the combined genotypes. It then translates that genetic code into physical phenotypes (the actual drawing of the seed!).
  • Telemetry: The right panel acts as your lab notebook, automatically tallying the physical results and calculating the final phenotypic ratio.
