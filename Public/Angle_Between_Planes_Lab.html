📘 ANGLE BETWEEN PLANES PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the 3D Geometry Laboratory! Analyze the spatial relationships and intersections of infinite planes.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Normal Vector Sliders: Adjust the $A, B, C$ coefficients for Plane 1 (Blue) and Plane 2 (Rose). This tilts the planes in 3D space.
  • Preset Dropdown: Instantly jump to mathematically significant states like Orthogonal (90°) or Parallel (0°).
  • Camera Controls: Click and drag on the 3D canvas to orbit around the intersection. Scroll to zoom.
  • Visual Toggles: Turn the Normal Vector arrows and the bright yellow Line of Intersection on or off to clarify the geometry.
