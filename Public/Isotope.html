📖 ISOTOPES virtual laboratory manual

Explore isotopic structures, nucleon stability limits, and dynamic mixture mass calculations:

1. Isotope Builder Sandbox

Select any element from the quick selection bar. Use Protons (+/-), Neutrons (+/-), and Electrons (+/-) steppers to assemble stable or unstable nuclei. Observe the spinning electron shells, nuclear core packed coordinates, and real-time radioactive shaking vibration for unstable isotopes.

2. Isotope Mixtures & Abundance

Switch to the Mixtures tab. Here you can add or subtract counts of different isotopes in a sample. Telemetry calculates the weighted average atomic mass of your customized mixture and represents it on a vectors-drawn physical balancing scale, matching it against the standard periodic table value.

3. Viva Voce Questions

Answer the 5 multiple choice questions on the right-hand desk. Each selection will lock in color-coded feedback. Click "Show Scientific Verification" at the bottom of each card to expand advanced LaTeX proofs compiled by MathJax!
