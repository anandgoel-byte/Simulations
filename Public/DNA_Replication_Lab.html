📘 DNA REPLICATION PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Molecular Genetics Laboratory! Observe the real-time mechanics of the replisome.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Enzyme Speed Slider: Control the playback speed. Slow it down to watch how the lagging strand polymerase has to continually jump backward to lay down new segments!
  • Leading vs Lagging: The top strand (Leading) is built continuously as the Helicase unzips the DNA. The bottom strand (Lagging) must be built in chunks (Okazaki Fragments) because it runs in the opposite direction.
  • Primer Replacement: Watch the Primase lay down red RNA primers. Then watch DNA Pol I swap them out, followed by Ligase sealing the gaps!
