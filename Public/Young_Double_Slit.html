📘 YOUNG'S DOUBLE SLIT - User Guide

Welcome to the Young's Double Slit Interference Virtual Lab! Explore wave optics with these interactive controls:

🎯 Core Controls

  • Wavelength Slider: Adjust the light color (from blue 400nm to red 700nm) to see how wavelength alters the fringe separation.
  • Slit Spacing Slider: Adjust the separation $d$ between S1 and S2.
  • Drag Screens: Drag Screen A or B horizontally on the canvas to adjust the screen-to-slit propagation distance $D$.
  • Spot Measurement Probe: Hover your mouse cursor over the canvas to dynamically view local path difference, phase difference, and alignment state.
