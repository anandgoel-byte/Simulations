📘 3D DISTANCE & SECTION PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the 3D Coordinate Laboratory! Explore the geometry of lines and ratios in three-dimensional space.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Coordinate Sliders: Adjust the $x,y,z$ positions of Points A and B.
  • Ratio Sliders ($m, n$): Adjust the ratio that determines where Point P splits the line.
  • Division Mode: Toggle between Internal (P is between A and B) and External (P lies outside the segment AB). Notice how the mathematical formula changes!
  • Camera Controls: Click and drag on the 3D canvas to orbit around the line. Scroll to zoom.

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Distance in 3D Space: Compute the distance between two 3D coordinate points using Cartesian components.
  • Internal & External Sectioning: Apply the section formula to locate partition points dividing segments internally or externally.
  • Ratio Proportionality: Visualize how ratio variables ($m, n$) physically translate partition points along the line segment.
