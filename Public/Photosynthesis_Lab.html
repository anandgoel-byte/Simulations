📘 PHOTOSYNTHESIS PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Biochemistry Laboratory! Discover how plants convert sunlight and air into energy.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Environment Presets: Quickly switch between Optimal conditions, Drought (no water), and Night time (no light).
  • Input Sliders: Manually adjust Light, Water, and Carbon Dioxide levels. Notice how a lack of water stops oxygen production, and a lack of light stops everything!
  • The Energy Pool: The Calvin Cycle (right side) depends on the ATP/NADPH generated by the Thylakoids (left side). If you turn off the light, watch the energy pool drain until sugar production halts completely.
