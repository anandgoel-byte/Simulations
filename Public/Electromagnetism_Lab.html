System Functions

  • Solenoid Mode: Observe the straight, uniform magnetic field lines passing through the center of the helix. Notice how they bend around the outside, causing "flux leakage" at the ends.
  • Toroid Mode: The solenoid is bent into a closed donut shape. Notice that the magnetic field lines form perfect, unbroken circles strictly confined inside the core. There is zero field leakage outside!
  • Current (I) & Turns (N): Increasing these values directly multiplies the strength of the B-field in both geometries. You can watch the animated electron particles speed up or become denser.
