📘 ODE PLOTTER PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Differential Equations Laboratory! Visualize the hidden currents of mathematics.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Equation Dropdown: Select a classic differential equation. Notice how $y' = -x/y$ creates a swirling whirlpool of circular orbits!
  • Initial Condition Sliders: Adjust $x_0$ and $y_0$ to drop a point into the vector field. The red solution curve will dynamically recalculate using RK4 integration to trace the flow forward and backward in time.
  • Canvas Interaction: You can click anywhere directly on the dark canvas to instantly move the initial condition point to that location!
