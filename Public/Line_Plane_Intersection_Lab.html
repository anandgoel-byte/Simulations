📘 LINE & PLANE INTERSECTION PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the 3D Coordinate Geometry Laboratory! Analyze the spatial algebra governing line-plane intersections.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Plane Sliders ($a, b, c, d$): Modify the normal vector coefficients to tilt the blue plane, and adjust $d$ to translate it through space.
  • Direction Sliders ($v_x, v_y, v_z$): Change the direction vector of the line to alter its orientation.
  • Base Point Sliders ($x_0, y_0, z_0$): Shift the anchor starting position of the line.
  • Camera Interactions: Click and drag inside the 3D canvas window to rotate your viewport perspective. Scroll to zoom in and out.
