📘 QUANTUM PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Atomic Spectral Lines Visualizer! Explore how electrons de-excite to produce unique photon emission spectra.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Excited State ($n$): Use the slider to set the highest energy level of the electron. The 3D reactor core will highlight the active disc.
  • Trigger Emission: Click to release the electrons! Watch them cascade down to lower energy states. Each distinct jump emits a unique spectral line.
  • The Telemetry: The dashboard automatically calculates the total lines using $n(n-1)/2$ and sorts them by series (Lyman = Purple, Balmer = Cyan, Paschen = Red).

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Energy Quantization: Comprehend that electrons exist in discrete, quantized energy levels within an atom.
  • De-excitation and Emission: Understand how transitions from higher excited states to lower states emit photons of specific wavelengths.
  • Spectral Series Identifier: Classify emission spectral lines into named series (Lyman, Balmer, Paschen) depending on their terminal ground state.
