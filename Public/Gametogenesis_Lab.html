📘 GAMETOGENESIS PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Human Reproduction Laboratory! Compare the cellular pathways that produce sperm and eggs.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Pathway Toggle: Switch between the Male (Spermatogenesis) and Female (Oogenesis) pathways. Notice how the visual tree structure drastically changes!
  • Developmental Slider: Drag the slider (or click the phase buttons) to step through Mitosis, Growth, Meiosis I, Meiosis II, and Differentiation.
  • Ploidy Tracking: Watch the Telemetry panel. Note exactly when the cell drops from 46 chromosomes (Diploid) to 23 chromosomes (Haploid).
  • Polar Bodies: In the female pathway, observe the unequal division of cytoplasm. The tiny offshoot cells are Polar Bodies, which exist solely to discard excess chromosomes while saving the nutrients for the Ovum!
