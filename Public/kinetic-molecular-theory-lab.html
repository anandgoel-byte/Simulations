📘 GAS LAWS & KMT - User Guide

Welcome to the Gas Properties & Kinetic Molecular Theory Lab! Explore the mechanical interactions of gas molecules in real-time under standard thermodynamics laws.

🎯 Core Tabs

  • Ideal Gas Sandbox: Watch multi-particle gas kinetics and toggle parameters freely. Set variable locks to observe Gay-Lussac's, Boyle's, or Charles's gas laws dynamically.
  • Speed Distributions: View average Maxwell-Boltzmann velocity metrics comparing heavy and light species under thermal equilibrium.

🔒 Hold Constant Locks

  • None (Free): Change Volume or Temperature, and watch Pressure update dynamically.
  • Volume (V): Locking volume disables the Width slider. Adjusting temperature dynamically modifies target pressure.
  • Temperature (T): Locking temperature disables temperature inputs. Adjusting pressure dynamically scales container width.
  • Pressure (P): Locking pressure disables pressure inputs. Adjusting temperature immediately shifts volume to maintain the target pressure.
