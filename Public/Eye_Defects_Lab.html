📘 OPTICS PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Eye Defects & Optics Simulator! Visualize how light interacts with the human eye and how physics corrects biological imperfections.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Select Condition: Choose a defect like Myopia or Cataract. Watch how the rays fail to converge properly on the retina.
  • Object Distance: Drag the red apple object directly on the canvas or slide the Object Distance slider. Notice how Myopia and Hypermetropia manifest at different distances!
  • Apply Correction: Click the glasses icon to drop in the correct lens (Concave, Convex, or Cylindrical) and watch the focal point snap back to the retina.
  • Persistence of Vision: Select this special mode at the bottom of the list. Spin the disc to see how your brain blends two separate images into one!

🎓 Learning Outcomes

  • Mechanism of Vision: Understand light refraction through the cornea and lens to focus an image on the retina.
  • Pathophysiology of Defects: Define focal displacements for Myopia, Hypermetropia, Astigmatism, and Presbyopia.
  • Correction Principles: Learn how diverging (concave), converging (convex), and cylindrical lenses correct focal placement.
  • Temporal Perception: Grasp the persistence of vision physics and thaumatrope principles.
