📘 LINKAGE & RECOMBINATION PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Genetics Laboratory! Explore how distance affects the inheritance of linked genes.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Gene Distance Slider: Adjust the physical distance between Gene A and Gene B on the chromosome. Notice how moving them further apart increases the probability of a chiasma forming between them!
  • Trigger Meiosis: Click the button to watch Prophase I. The inner sister chromatids will cross over. The final result splits into 4 separate haploid gametes (sperm/egg).
  • The 50% Rule: Notice that even at maximum distance (50 cM), the recombination frequency maxes out at 50%. This is because the outer two chromatids never participate in the crossover, ensuring at least 50% of the gametes are always "Parental".
