📘 PROJECTILE BALLISTICS LAB PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Interactive Projectile Kinematics & Aerodynamics Virtual Laboratory! Explore physical flight kinematics, non-linear air resistance, and measurement vector fields.

🎯 Sandbox Controls & Parameters

  • Fire Cannon: Fires a projectile according to your parameter configuration.
  • Clear Trajectories: Clears all rendered curves, milestones, and flight data history.
  • Launch Parameters: Adjust the Initial Velocity ($v_0$), Launch Height ($h$), and Launch Angle ($\theta$) dynamically using either range sliders or numerical inputs.
  • Archetypes & Custom Settings: Select a projectile preset (e.g. Baseball, Cannonball) or customize its Mass ($m$) and Diameter ($d$) manually.
  • Air Resistance (Drag): Enable aerodynamics to simulate fluid drag using quadratic equations based on air density $\rho = 1.204\text{ kg/m}^3$ and the drag coefficient ($C_d$).

📏 Measurement & Probing Tools

  • Tape Measure: Click to activate, then drag the start (orange square) and end (red crosshair) targets to measure precise distance, horizontal displacement ($\Delta x$), and vertical height ($\Delta y$).
  • Inspect Probe Tool: Click to activate, then drag the inspection crosshair near any trajectory milestone dot to snap and view exact time, height, and range telemetry.

📊 Live Telemetry & Verification

  • Live Telemetry: Tracks Flight Time, Apogee (maximum height), and Range of the last fired projectile.
  • Theoretical Path Guide: Check the box to overlay the ideal theoretical path ($45^\circ$ angle with no air resistance) as a reference comparison.
  • Vector Visualizations: Display real-time Velocity and Acceleration vectors as arrows on the flying projectile.

📝 Conceptual Assessment

  • Test your knowledge of kinematic equations, range, and times of flight at the **Viva Voce Lab Desk** with real-time interactive grading.
