📘 LENS REFRACTION PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Refraction through Lenses Virtual Laboratory! Interact with converging and diverging systems to learn how ray diagrams are constructed.

🎯 Drag and Drop Mechanics

  • The Object (Blue Arrow): Drag the white circle at the tip of the blue arrow to dynamically adjust both the Object Distance ($u$) and the Object Height ($h_o$).
  • The Focal Points (F₁ / F₂): Click and drag either focal point handle along the principal axis to increase or decrease the lens' focal length ($f$).

🔬 Key Sign Conventions

  • Lens Formula: $\frac{1}{v} - \frac{1}{u} = \frac{1}{f}$.
  • The object is always placed on the left side of the lens, so $u$ is mathematically negative.
  • Convex lens has a positive focal length ($f > 0$), whereas concave lens has a negative focal length ($f < 0$).
