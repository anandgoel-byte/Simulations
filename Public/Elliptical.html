📘 ELLIPTICAL ORBITS & KEPLER'S 2ND LAW - User Guide

Welcome to the ELLIPTICAL ORBITS & Kepler's 2nd Law Virtual Lab! Use this simulation to explore central-force celestial mechanics.

🎯 Core Functions

  • Run / Pause / Reset: Use the top bar buttons to control physical time propagation.
  • Interactive Parameters: Adjust the initial orbital distance ($r_0$), tangential launch speed ($v_0$), or Sun mass ($M_S$) to observe different conic sections.
  • Orbit Shape Predictor: Instantly classifies the path as a Circle, Ellipse, Parabola, or Hyperbola based on specific orbital energy state.
  • Kepler's 2nd Law Trace: Enables equal-time wedge sweeps. Notice how the swept areas remain identical regardless of whether the planet is near perihelion (fast, short wide wedges) or aphelion (slow, long narrow wedges).

🔍 Canvas Controls

  • Zoom In / Out / Recenter: Adjust your spatial coordinate field of view.
  • Drag to Pan: Click and drag anywhere on the canvas to inspect the grid.
  • Show Grid & Axes: Toggle coordinate system axes lines and grid ticks.
