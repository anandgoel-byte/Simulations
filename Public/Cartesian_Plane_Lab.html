📘 CARTESIAN GRID PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Cartesian Plane Simulator. This grid is how mathematicians assign coordinates to geometric space!

🎯 Core Controls

  • The Origin: Right in the center is $(0,0)$. This is where we start all our measurements.
  • Abscissa (X): Move the X slider. The cyan line shows your distance left or right from the center.
  • Ordinate (Y): Move the Y slider. The pink line shows your distance up or down from the center.
  • The Quadrants: Notice how the four corners light up depending on whether your numbers are positive or negative. Quadrant I is $(+,+)$, Quadrant II is $(-,+)$, Quadrant III is $(-,-)$, and Quadrant IV is $(+,-)$.
