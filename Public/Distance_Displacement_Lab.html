Kinematic Definitions
Distance Scalar
The total length of the actual path covered by the moving object. Depends entirely on the route taken.
Formula: $\text{Distance} = \sum \Delta s_i$
Displacement Vector
The shortest straight-line distance from the initial position to the final position.
Formula: $\vec{s} = \vec{r}_f - \vec{r}_i$
