📘 DIODE CHARACTERISTICS PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the p-n Junction Diode Virtual Laboratory! Explore the non-linear relationship between voltage and current in semiconductors.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Power Supply ON: Activates the circuit and allows the voltage slider to dictate current flow.
  • Bias Voltage Slider: Drag right to apply Forward Bias (reducing the barrier). Drag left to apply Reverse Bias (widening the depletion region).
  • Material Selection: Compare standard Silicon (0.7V knee) with Germanium (0.3V knee). Watch how the entire I-V curve shifts!
  • Dual Viewport: The left side of the canvas illustrates the physical semiconductor lattice and the central depletion region. The right side plots the mathematical I-V curve in real-time.
