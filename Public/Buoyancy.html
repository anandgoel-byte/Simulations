📘 BUOYANCY PROTOCOLS - User Guide

Welcome to the Buoyancy & Fluid Displacement Virtual Laboratory! Explore the fluid dynamics, Archimedes conditions, and FBD vector parameters.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Parameter Syncing: Synced sliders or manual inputs control Object Density ($\rho_o$) and Fluid Density ($\rho_f$).
  • Chamber Viscosity: Adjust viscosity to modify drag, altering bob terminal sink rates.
  • FBD Overlay HUD: Toggle to render a live, vector-drawn Free-Body Diagram overlaid directly inside the chamber canvas.
  • Live Telemetry: Track buoyant force ($F_b$), weight ($W$), submerged ratio, and displacement conditions in real time.
