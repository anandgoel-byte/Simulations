📘 NATURAL SELECTION PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Evolution & Population Genetics Lab! Observe how environmental pressures physically alter the genetic makeup of a population over time.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Environmental Background: Slide from a light birch forest to a soot-covered dark forest. Notice how the canvas background physically changes color.
  • Predator Pressure: This dictates how aggressively birds eat the moths that stand out. If set to 0%, no one is eaten, and Hardy-Weinberg equilibrium is perfectly maintained!
  • The Graph & Telemetry: As you step through generations, watch the real-time line graph plotting the frequency of the Dark Allele ($p$) versus the Light Allele ($q$). Notice how the physical phenotype frequencies (Dark vs Light) are fundamentally tied to the underlying $p^2$, $2pq$, and $q^2$ genotypes!
