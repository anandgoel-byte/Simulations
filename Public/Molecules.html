📘 MOLECULAR GEOMETRY & VSEPR - User Guide

Welcome to the Molecular Shapes & VSEPR 3D Virtual Lab! Use this simulation to analyze coordinate orbital spatial geometries under electrostatic repulsions.

🎯 Core Modes

  • Real Molecules: Select actual molecular systems (like $H_2O$, $CO_2$, $ClF_3$, or $XeF_4$) from the top toolbar select menu. View their experimental geometries, hybridized valence models, and scientific explanations.
  • Molecule Builder Sandbox: Click the Sandbox tab to build custom geometries. Dynamically add single, double, triple, or lone electron domains to watch VSEPR adapt in real-time up to the standard steric capacity limit of 6.

🔍 Spatial Navigation

  • 3D Orbit Rotation: Simply click and drag anywhere inside the black 3D canvas viewport to rotate the molecule. Observe spatial repulsions from any direction.
  • Display Toggles: Toggle "Show Bond Angles", "Show Lone Pairs", and "Show Geometry Labels" dynamically using the top checkboxes to refine your analysis.
