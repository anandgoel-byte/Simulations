📘 pH & INDICATOR PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Colorimetry & pH Laboratory! Explore the logarithmic scale of acidity.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Test Solutions: Use the top dropdown to swap between everyday liquids. Watch the pH meter calculate the exact value and the solution physically change color based on the active indicator.
  • Manual pH Slider: Want to test the exact transition point of an indicator? Grab the manual slider and sweep it from 0 to 14!
  • Indicators & The Gradient Bar: Different indicators are useful for different tasks. Notice the gradient bar at the bottom of the canvas—it provides a map of the current indicator's color profile across all 14 pH levels.
  • Telemetry Panel: Because pH is logarithmic ($-\log[H^+]$), a drop from pH 6 to pH 5 means the $[H^+]$ concentration has increased by a factor of 10! Watch the scientific notation update live.
