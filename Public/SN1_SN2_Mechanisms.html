📘 REACTION KINETICS PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Nucleophilic Substitution Virtual Laboratory! Explore the competitive battle between Sₙ1 and Sₙ2 pathways.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Substrate: Alter the steric hindrance. Methyl and 1° favor Sₙ2. 3° forces Sₙ1.
  • Nucleophile & Solvent: Tweaking these shifts the balance for Secondary (2°) substrates. Strong Nu/Aprotic pushes Sₙ2. Weak Nu/Protic pushes Sₙ1.
  • Stereochemical Rendering: Notice the proper 3D tetrahedral geometry! Solid lines are in-plane, Thick Wedges point toward you, and Dashes point away.
  • Animation: Watch the physical difference! Sₙ2 flips the molecule like an umbrella in one fluid step (Walden Inversion). Sₙ1 breaks apart, forms a flat $sp^2$ intermediate, and attacks.
