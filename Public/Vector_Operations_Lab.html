📘 VECTOR OPERATIONS PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Linear Algebra Laboratory! Visualize the mechanics of vectors in 3D space.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Vector Sliders: Adjust the individual $x,y,z$ components of Vectors $\vec{u}$ and $\vec{v}$.
  • Operation Dropdown:
    • Addition: Draws the resultant vector and completing parallelogram.
    • Dot Product: Draws a dashed line showing the geometric projection.
    • Cross Product: Generates the orthogonal vector and shades the spanning area.
  • Camera Controls: Click and drag on the 3D canvas to orbit around the vectors. Scroll to zoom.
