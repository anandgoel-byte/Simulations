📘 JK FLIP-FLOP PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Sequential Logic Virtual Laboratory! Explore how digital circuits store memory and respond to clock signals.

🎯 Core Controls

  • J & K Inputs: Click the large buttons to toggle the data lines High (1) or Low (0).
  • Clock Frequency: Adjust how fast the system evaluates states. Slower speeds make it easier to see exactly when the edge triggers.
  • Edge Triggering: Notice that changing J or K does not immediately change Q! The output only updates precisely when the Clock transitions from Low to High (Positive Edge).
  • The Oscilloscope: The bottom half of the canvas acts as a digital logic analyzer, plotting the real-time waveforms of all signals.
