Kinematic Algorithms
Avg Speed Scalar
The total distance covered by the moving object divided by the total elapsed time.
$$v_{\text{avg}} = \frac{\text{Total Distance}}{\text{Total Time}}$$
Avg Velocity Vector
The net displacement of the object divided by the total time. Depends on the direct change in position.
$$\vec{v}_{\text{avg}} = \frac{\text{Net Displacement}}{\text{Total Time}}$$
