📘 EM INDUCTION PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Electromagnetism Laboratory! Explore how physical movement can be converted directly into electrical energy.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Manual Dragging: You can click and drag the bar magnet horizontally across the canvas. Notice that the faster you drag it through the coil, the wilder the galvanometer needle swings!
  • Auto-Thrust: Click the play button to let the machine oscillate the magnet back and forth at a constant speed (adjustable via slider).
  • Faraday's Multipliers: Increase the Magnet Strength ($B$) or the Coil Turns ($N$). Watch the mathematical readout on the right dynamically multiply the voltage output!
  • Lenz's Law in Action: Flip the Magnet Poles. Notice that pushing the North pole into the coil creates a current in one direction, while pushing the South pole in creates a current in the exact opposite direction.
