📘 C3 vs C4 LABORATORY PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Plant Physiology Laboratory! Compare the metabolic strategies plants use to survive extreme climates.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Specimen Selector: Toggle between a standard C3 leaf (like wheat) and a specialized C4 leaf (like corn with Kranz Anatomy). Notice how the C4 plant has an extra ring of Bundle Sheath cells tightly surrounding the vascular vein.
  • Temperature Slider: As you increase the heat, watch the Stomata Guard Cells close up to prevent the plant from drying out.
  • Molecular Flow: When stomata close, oxygen gas ($O_2$) from light reactions cannot escape. In a C3 plant, watch the Rubisco enzyme choke on this $O_2$ (turning red), causing the Photorespiration rate to skyrocket while sugar production plummets.
  • C4 Survival: Switch to the C4 plant at high heat. Notice how PEP Carboxylase completely ignores the trapped $O_2$, pumping carbon safely into the inner Bundle Sheath cells where Rubisco can work in peace!
