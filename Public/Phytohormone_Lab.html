📘 PHYTOHORMONE PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Botanical Laboratory! Discover how chemical signals shape physical plant architecture.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Hormone Sliders: Adjust the levels of the 5 major phytohormones. The plant will dynamically redraw itself based on the competing chemical ratios.
  • Auxin vs Cytokinin: Watch how high Auxin creates a tall, single-stem plant with deep roots. Raise Cytokinin to break this dominance and force wide lateral branching!
  • Ethylene & ABA: Increase Ethylene to watch the fruit turn red and the leaves begin to drop. Increase ABA to simulate stress, halting growth and stunting the plant.
