📘 TRIGONOMETRY PROTOCOLS

Welcome to the Heights & Distances 3D Simulator! Visualize how trigonometric ratios link physical distances to angles.

🎯 Core Controls

  • Altitude ($H$): This is the Opposite side of the right-angled triangle. Adjusting it physically moves the plane up and down.
  • Ground Distance ($D$): This is the Adjacent side. Adjusting it moves the plane left and right relative to the observer.
  • Angle of Elevation ($\theta$): Automatically calculated. Notice that when Altitude = Distance, the angle is exactly $45^\circ$!
  • Line of Sight: This is the Hypotenuse. Watch the Live Telemetry panel recalculate Sine, Cosine, and Tangent dynamically as the 3D geometry changes.
